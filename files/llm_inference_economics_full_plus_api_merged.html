E. Verdict

Can $20 paid subs subsidize free chat usage?

  • OpenAI (ChatGPT): Gross profit (opt‑mid) = $49,457,030 ⇒ profit after free is included. Break‑even ≈ $17.41/mo.
  • Anthropic (Claude): Gross profit (opt‑mid) = $6,344,304 ⇒ profit with scaled free costs.
  • API: Per‑million unit margins are generally positive at list prices vs our cost range, suggesting API can cross‑subsidize chat if needed.

Figures are serving COGS only (GPUs, power, cooling, colocation); exclude staff, R&D, networking/egress, training amortization, idle capacity.
